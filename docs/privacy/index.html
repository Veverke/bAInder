bAInder Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-16
bAInder stores your saved data locally in your browser and does not send your chat archive to bAInder-operated servers.

Summary

  • No analytics and no telemetry.
  • No account required.
  • Saved data is stored in chrome.storage.local.
  • No sale of personal data.

Data Accessed

bAInder accesses conversation data on supported AI chat pages so you can save, organize, search, and compare chats within the extension.

  • Chat messages and titles
  • Metadata required for display and sorting
  • Image URLs and image data required for saved artifacts

Data Storage

Saved content is stored locally in your browser via chrome.storage.local. bAInder does not upload your saved archive to bAInder servers.

Permissions and Purpose

Extension permissions are used for local organization features:

  • storage, unlimitedStorage: local archive storage.
  • tabs, activeTab, scripting: capture and compare interactions on supported tabs.
  • sidePanel: extension UI in the browser side panel.
  • contextMenus: right-click actions.
  • alarms: local scheduled tasks.

Host permissions are limited to supported chat providers and related image hosts:

  • OpenAI/ChatGPT: chat.openai.com, chatgpt.com, files.oaiusercontent.com, oaidalleapiprodscus.blob.core.windows.net, oaidalleaeuropeprodscus.blob.core.windows.net
  • Anthropic Claude: claude.ai
  • Google Gemini: gemini.google.com, lh3.google.com, lh3.googleusercontent.com
  • Microsoft Copilot: copilot.microsoft.com, m365.cloud.microsoft, th.bing.com, www.bing.com
  • Perplexity: www.perplexity.ai, perplexity.ai

Data Sharing

bAInder does not sell or rent your data. Data is only accessed as needed to perform extension features in your browser context.

Children's Privacy

bAInder is not intended for children under 13.
